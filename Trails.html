Guests at our cottages get access to kilometers of walking trails. The trail showcases Ontario's diverse forestry and landscape. Explore a network that connects you to Big Chute, OFSC trails, and other private roadways.

Expect a peaceful and serene walk with moderate hills and exciting moments like walking over top a beaver dam. You will also get to see historical artifacts that were part of the areas logging efforts in the 19th century

The trails are perfect for taking the dog on a walk or foraging. Please note the best time to use the trails are May - June and Sep - Oct to avoid the flies.

Caution: Trails are not marked, it is recommend trail hikers bring water, a compass, cellphone and ensure enough time to make it home before it gets dark. It takes aprox 1.5 hrs to walk to big chute and back. There are wild animals in these forests. You may come across deer, moose, bears, owls, racoons, otters, beavers, porcupines, foxes and coyotes.
